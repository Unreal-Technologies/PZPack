 Ideally situated between the towns of Muldraugh and West Point, Dixie Trailer Park offers multiple amenities to satisfy your resting needs.
 Refuel your car and your body at the local gas station and grocery store. Looking to freshen up? Check out the local laundromat or our clean public restrooms!
 If you're just looking for a quick bite, head on over to the diner. The locals say the food is simply to die for!
